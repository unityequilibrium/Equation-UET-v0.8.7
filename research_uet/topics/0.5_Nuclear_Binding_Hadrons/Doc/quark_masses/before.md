# Before: Quark Masses

## ปัญหา (Limitation)
Quark mass hierarchy:
- u,d: ~2-5 MeV
- s: ~95 MeV
- c: ~1.3 GeV
- b: ~4.2 GeV
- t: ~173 GeV

Why this pattern?

## ข้อจำกัดของทฤษฎีเดิม
- SM has 6 Yukawa couplings (free parameters)
- No prediction from QCD
- Hierarchy unexplained
